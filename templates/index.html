Hotel Inforfation

Our website serves as a vital platform for hotels in the area to communicate their surplus food availability, facilitating the connection between excess provisions and those in need. By leveraging this digital network, we strive to bridge the gap between abundance and scarcity, aiming to eradicate hunger from our communities. Through our collective efforts, we endeavor to make strides towards a world where no one goes to bed hungry.

Location

Our website offers a unique feature allowing users to share their current location, enabling us to track surplus food availability in their vicinity. By voluntarily providing their location, individuals can contribute to our mission of redistributing excess food to those in need. Through our platform, we endeavor to bridge the gap between food wastage and hunger by efficiently connecting donors with local communities in need. Join us in our efforts to make a meaningful impact by sharing your location and helping us reach out to those who can benefit from your generosity.
